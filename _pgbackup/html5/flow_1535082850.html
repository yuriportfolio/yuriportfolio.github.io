My work started

when woodworking company decided to hire me as a designer on order to 

move from a dead point in their production.


The business problem 

 First of all it was hard to create something new, machines were working all the time and 

the manufactory worked only with wood. Nobody had vision in what shouod be changed 

to make more money with less working power.

Solution

The answer was in implementing other materials in new designs.

This was something fresh and my designs immidiatelly had succes in scandinavian furniture market.

Interested to see them?

First try

this was a great design which started with an idia to use metall in combination with wood so after some scetches I made, I found partners who made this possible.

more designs 

After this succes, I created a lot of collections. Our clients preffered this new "metall vision" orders on this type of furniture were growing.

After all

this took our sales on a new level
